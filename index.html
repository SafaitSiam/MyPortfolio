About Me

Every frame
tells a story.

With five years behind the timeline, I've crafted videos for brands, content creators, agencies, and filmmakers. From raw footage to final delivery — I handle the full post-production pipeline with precision and creative intent.

My primary craft is video editing — finding the rhythm in footage, shaping narratives through cuts and pacing, and delivering polished final products. I also create motion graphics and animated elements that elevate the visual storytelling.

I work across Premiere Pro, After Effects, and DaVinci Resolve. Whether it's a brand campaign, YouTube content, a documentary, or social media reels — I bring the same level of care and attention to every project.
